
# Inventory System – Clarification Questions

To design the right system for your business, we need clarity on a few points below.

---

## 1. Business & Setup

1. Do you already have **existing stock** in warehouses or branches that must be added at the start?
2. Do you have existing data in **Excel or another system** that needs to be imported?

---

## 2. Products & Barcodes

3. Do products have **sizes or colors** (for example: T-Shirt – S, M, L in different colors)?
4. Should **each size/color** be treated as a **separate item**?
5. Should **every item** have a **barcode**, or only some items?
6. Can one product have **multiple barcodes** (for example: single item + box of 12)?
7. Do you want to add **product photos**?

---

## 3. Barcodes & Scanning

8. How will you **scan barcodes**?
   * USB barcode scanner (plugged into computer)
   * Mobile phone camera
   * Both
9. Do you have **barcode label printers**? If yes, what label size?

---

## 4. Stock Entry & Movement

10. How does new stock enter the system?
    * Direct entry at warehouse
    * From your own production
11. Can **branches request stock**, or is stock sent only by head office?
12. Can stock be transferred **between branches**, or only from warehouse to branch?
13. Do stock transfers need **approval** before sending?
14. Can a transfer be sent **in parts** (for example: 50 items today, 50 tomorrow)?
15. If branch receives **fewer items** than sent (damaged or lost), what should happen?

---

## 5. Warehouses & Branches

16. Can a **branch also act like a warehouse**, or are they separate?
17. Should branches have **maximum stock limits** (storage capacity)?

---

## 6. Transactional Items & Invoices

18. Which items **require invoices** (for example: stationery, papers, etc.)?
19. Is **invoice creation mandatory** even if payment is not received?
20. Do invoices need:
    * Tax / VAT?
    * Due dates?
21. Can invoices be **paid in parts** (installments)?
22. Can invoices be **edited after creation**, or are they final?
23. If items are returned or damaged, what happens?

---

## 7. Pricing

24. Can prices change **per invoice**, or are they fixed?
25. Do you use **one currency only**?

---

## 8. Users & Access

26. What user roles do you need?
    * Admin
    * Warehouse staff
    * Branch staff
    * Accounts
    * Other: _______________
27. Should branch/warehouse users **only see their own location's data**?

---

## 9. Reports

28. What reports are most important?
    * Stock per warehouse
    * Stock per branch
    * Transfer history
    * Invoice reports
    * Other: _______________
29. Do you need reports in **Excel, PDF, or both**?

---
